Project Description

The producer-merchant relationship reinvented. NectarGo is a digital tool that allows producers and merchants to work better and directly for a more local and sustainable food. It is an application that brings together a community of local partners, reinforced by a support team close to you. NectarGo allows you to manage your direct orders at once and very simply, optimize store deliveries, simplify your administrative procedures.
